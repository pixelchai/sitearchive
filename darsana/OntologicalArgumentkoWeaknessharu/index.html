Anselm ko God exist gurcha bhaneko argument ko weaknesses haru

23 Feb 2016 on Darsana

Guanilo

Guanilo bhaneko sabhanda paileko critic thyo - Anselm ko Ontological Argument ma. Guanilo le euta fault specify gardena tara egdum generic cha bhantheyo: “God” bhaneko ze ni le replace gurna sukcha.

Maile che euta hasilo kura soche: “Greatest Cookie Possible” - “The Cookie than which no greater can exist”. Euta “subunda dherei sakthi bhako cookie” socha. Aba, yo cookie bhaneko hajur ko immagination ma matrai haina tara yo pirthibi ma ni exist hunthyo bhane - zuhn pani sukthi huntho hai? (yo ho Anselm le bhaneko kura). Anselm ko bhanai ma, euta thuuuuulo cookie yo pithibi ma cha. Tara - yo thuuli cookie zahn pani ramro (sukthii bhako) hunthyo hamrai ughadi thyo bhane, hai? Anselm ko bhanai ma, yo right ho. Khasto funtus!

Yei kura Guanilo le bhaneko theyo (tara cookie ko sutha ma euta island ko barema bhaneko theyo).

Yo bhandemapani, yo bhaneko Anselm ko argument ko euta weaker argument ho kina bhane “subunda dherei sukthi bhako” bhaneko egdum subjective ho. For example, mero bhanai ma maile “yo hamro ughadi theyo bhane zuhn ramro hunto” bhandakheri - ramro bhaneko egdum subjective ho: Tyo cookie hamro ughadi theyo bhane hamro lagi ramro huntheyo - tara - thyo bichara cookie lai nuramro huntheyo.
